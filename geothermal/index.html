Geothermal Energy

The earth is constantly changing. It is moving and interacting with itself, and at its center is hot, molten rock. This is a lot of heat energy created every day, and it can be accessed by humans and used to heat our homes.

Geothermal energy is available anywhere in the world. It can be tapped from the Earth using heat pump technologies.

These pumps only have to go a few feet underground to access the constant temperature of the Earth. It remains about the same in the hottest summers and coldest winters.

Geothermal energy is considered renewable as there is no shortage of the heat coming from the Earth, and using it is a green solution to fossil fuels.

This uses a heat pump that goes a few feet down into the Earth’s surface and then takes advantage of the temperature differences between the air and the ground. These circulate the cold air outside with the warm air underground to warm a home.

Geothermal only uses a little electricity to circulate the air but is otherwise free. It is a great investment for anyone who is interested in going green or saving money.
